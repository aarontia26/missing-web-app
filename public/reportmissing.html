Lost and Found

Growing numbers of cases about a missing person, especially after the typhoons and flooding that we are all experiencing, numerous cases cannot solve because of lack of information to communicate with the family of the missing person. This web application will lessen those public issues about missing persons that turn into leftover cases. In this web application you can register all your family member, browse to the cases of missing person that was posted to the bulletin board of the web app and if you have information about the location of the missing person, you can directly communicate to the family member of the person by the information corresponds to the posted missing person.
